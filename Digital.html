Now days all works in marketing industry is done through digital ways. If you are a business owner who wants to improve your online business or a Fresher who wants to make career in digital marketing or develop an additional skill, this course is perfect for you. This Specialization will help you provide excellent knowledge, planning techniques, how to implement marketing campaigns using latest digital tools. The course will be handled overall by digital marketing professionals from Best industries.

The world is now online. This has made digital marketing channels and social media the most widespread, interactive and essential of all modern marketing tools. Any effective marketing campaign will include them in its strategy. Digital Marketing can be termed as a strategy or a perfect marketing exercise using digital platforms. It is believed that Digital Marketing is now the most crucial part of the marketing World. It has been grown rapidly in the past few years as more and more businesses are realizing the importance of a good online presentation. Digital Marketing includes many advertising channels including SEO, email marketing, social Media marketing, pay per click advertising, Google AdWords, mobile marketing, referral marketing, etc.

 

Objectives

  • At the end of this course, you will understand the various channels and activities required to plan, implement digital marketing plan for your business.
  • Gain a clear understanding of the value of Digital Marketing and why it is important component of your overall marketing strategy.
  • Plan, create, manage your own Search, Social Media, Mobile and Email Marketing campaigns.
  • Understand how Digital Marketing can help you grow your business, domestically and internationally.
  • Identify various online applications and resources that can help build effective and profitable websites.
  • Measure and analyze the visitor traffic to your website to continuously develop and improve your Digital Marketing efforts.

Career Opportunities

You can work as Digital Marketing Manager, SEO Manager, SEM Specialist and Digital Sales Executive, Social Media, Email Marketing, Web Analytics etc, work as a Freelancer or support your own business. The job description of a Digital Marketing manager is very broad, and often varies from organization to organization. And it is the best hired job in today’s digital world.
