About Us

BLOOMZEN INFOSOLUTIONS PVT LTD

21st century has many shades in terms of developing countries like India. Information Technology is the one which not only made the world closer but also created multiple growth opportunities in terms of making easeness of day to day lifestyle of an individual by using the technology application. It also created huge career opportunities in the field of Information Technology.

On the other hand, many IT aspirants who are in need of (SWOT) analysis decide the career move which can actually help them to make the stable career in their area of interest. Bloomzen Infosolutions Private Limited was incorporated in August 2020 with the motive of providing various career courses and creating job opportunities to the young talents at affordable cost. The founder has rich experience in career counseling and interaction with Industry – Academia relationship.

We at Bloomzen Infosolutions Pvt Ltd, with our unique strategy for ‘Train & deploy program’, We select deserving candidate on the basis of SWOT analysis and train them on tailor-made training (as per our clients requirement) on various job oriented courses like, Full Stack Development, Web development, Machine learning, Android /IOS application development, software Testing and Digital Marketing and after the successful completion of training we help them to get deploy at our clients location.
Our tailor-made career programs will not only help to the young talents to get their first break but also it helps to reduce the recruitment cost for industry ready candidates.

Today, many IT firms are facing one common issue of getting right resources at right positions with required skill set (Technical, aptitude and attitude), and it’s a very crucial process for the recruiters to select the best of the best talent during the campus recruitment not only because of the gap between the academic syllabus and industry expectations but due to rapid changes like integrated technologies, tailor-made requirements of the recruiters but also a matter of soft skills of the candidate.

 

Bloomzen Infosolutions incorporated in the year August 2020. Aiming at proving various skill development program to the young IT aspirants and nurturing their talents through practical based learning approach and tailor made programs as per the current industry trends.

Our industry experts are always passionate about sharing their practical work experience based on machine test evaluation enables them to become the job ready professional and easily crack the placement process.
