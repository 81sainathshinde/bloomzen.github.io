What is ISTQB?

ISTQB Certification is an internationally accepted software testing certification that is conducted online by its
Member Boards through a testing Exam Provider.

The importance of ISTQB certification:

  • There is no minimum qualification or experience needed to take the Foundation Level exam.
  • Increase their marketability throughout the industry.
  • Can add the “ISTQB® Certified Tester” logo and credential to their resumes.
  • ISTQB certification makes you eligible to get the testing job anywhere in this world unlikely to the other certifications which are recognized country wise.
  • ISTQB certification will not expire.
